About Alianca

Alianca Logistics is one of the best logistics companies in India, ventured in 2001 by Mr. Raman Garkhel, as a non-asset-based 3PL Solution Provider.

We have a vast network of most reliable International Freight Brokers across the globe. We have partnered with different professionals to offer value added services to the logistics industries. With the better access and network, we strive to offer solutions targeted at improving productivity and helping companies to reach their goals.

We have expertise in providing freight services like Cargo Pickup from Origin, Preparation of all Documents, Custom Clearance & Formalities, Cargo Forwarding & Space Reservation, Hazardous Cargo Handling, Handling of Cargo Having Stringent Temperature Requirements, Door Delivery to the Destination, Cargo Insurance, Export & Import Consolidation (Both Air & Sea).
