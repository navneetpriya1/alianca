What is a Third Party Logistics (3PL) Service?

A 3PL is an outsourced Logistics and freight service provided by a company that specializes in transporting, warehousing and scheduling of the delivery of goods owned by others. A 3PL service provider takes the physical possession of the goods to be delivered, arranges the appropriate delivery method or mode of transportation, arranges schedules and handles all operational tasks associated with the delivery process. These services may be provided on a one-time or ongoing basis.

Why do companies use 3PLs?

The benefit for companies to use a 3PL service provider is to save time and money. A 3PL service is a platform which saves companies from the hassle of managing their own fleets. A 3PL services provider offers large networks, flexibility, industry expertise and knowledge and better technology capabilities.
